Hi 😊

My name is Josh Jacob, owner and creator of Jacob Creative. I have a degree in Computer Science from Wilfrid Laurier University and have been coding for over 20 years. I’m an avid gamer, Leafs fan and cat dad to three.

In my corporate and small business web development experience, I’ve learned that most clients don’t know what exactly they’re signing up for when they sign a contract for a website to be created, and often the smaller business is put on a lower priority than large clients. I want to change that. I feel it's important to explain what you’re getting in simple language, so you understand what you’re paying for. I also believe each client deserves my focus and time. I enjoy working with small businesses that are looking to grow through using an online platform! Whether you have never had a website, or you’re needing a redesign of your current site or brand, I want to help make it an enjoyable experience that drives more people to your business.
